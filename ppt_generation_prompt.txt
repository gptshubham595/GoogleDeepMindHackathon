# PPT Generation Prompt for RAKSHAK - Digital Arrest Extortion Detection System

## Context
Create a 3-4 slide marketing presentation for RAKSHAK, an AI-powered mobile app that protects users from digital arrest extortion scams in India. The app uses real-time call monitoring with Google Gemini AI to detect scam calls and alert users instantly.

## Slide 1: Hero/Introduction Slide
**Title:** RAKSHAK - Your Shield Against Digital Arrest Scams
**Subtitle:** AI-Powered Real-Time Scam Call Detection for India

**Banner Design:**
- Modern, tech-forward design with gradient background (dark blue to purple)
- Central visual: Smartphone with shield icon protecting against scam call alerts
- Tagline: "Stop Scams Before They Start"
- Key stat: "Protecting millions from digital arrest extortion"

**Content:**
- **Problem:** Digital arrest scams cost Indians ₹1,000+ crores annually. Scammers impersonate police, customs, or government officials to extort money.
- **Solution:** RAKSHAK uses advanced AI to monitor calls in real-time, detect scam patterns, and alert users instantly.
- **Target:** 500+ million smartphone users in India vulnerable to these scams
- **Impact:** Prevent financial loss, protect vulnerable citizens, build trust in digital communication

## Slide 2: Technology & Features
**Title:** How RAKSHAK Works - AI-Powered Protection
**Subtitle:** Multi-Language Real-Time Threat Detection

**Banner Design:**
- Split screen: Left side shows Android app UI mockup, right side shows AI processing flow
- Icons for key features: Call monitoring, AI analysis, Alert system, Evidence collection
- Color scheme: Clean white background with accent colors for each feature

**Content - Core Features:**
1. **Real-Time Call Monitoring**
   - WebSocket-based live audio streaming to backend
   - Instant transcription using Speech-to-Text (STT)
   - Multi-language support: Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi

2. **AI-Powered Threat Detection**
   - Google Gemini AI models (gemini-3.1-flash-live-preview, gemini-3.5-flash)
   - Dual-layer detection: Pattern matching + LLM semantic analysis
   - Identifies scam patterns: fake police, customs, government impersonation

3. **Instant Voice Alerts**
   - Text-to-Speech (TTS) warnings in user's language
   - Voice styles: urgent, calm, clear, emphatic
   - Real-time audio feedback during suspicious calls

4. **Evidence Generation**
   - Automatic transcript collection
   - Cybercrime.gov.in ready complaint reports
   - One-tap evidence export for legal action

**Tech Stack:**
- **Android:** Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Firebase Auth/Firestore/Messaging, Hilt DI, Room DB, Retrofit
- **Backend:** FastAPI, Python, WebSocket, Google Gemini AI
- **AI Models:** Gemini Live API for real-time processing, Gemini 3.5 Flash for reasoning
- **On-Device:** TensorFlow Lite for offline fallback classification
